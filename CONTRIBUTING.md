# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to PulmoRehab Kit.

## Ways to contribute
You can help by:
- improving documentation
- suggesting new rehabilitation templates
- reporting bugs or unclear instructions
- proposing small workflow tools
- improving bilingual accessibility

## Before making major changes
Please open an issue first to discuss major changes, new modules, or structural updates.

## Contribution style
We welcome practical, lightweight, and reusable contributions that support pulmonary rehabilitation education and simple workflow tools.
