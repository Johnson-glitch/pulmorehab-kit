# Rehab Log Field Definitions

This document explains the starter fields used in `templates/rehab-daily-log.csv`.

## Field definitions

### date
Date of the rehab log entry.

### patient_id
A simple identifier for the patient or participant.

### breathing_exercise_sessions
Number of breathing exercise sessions completed on that day.

### walking_minutes
Total walking time in minutes.

### cough_training_sessions
Number of cough or airway clearance practice sessions completed.

### sputum_clearance_done
Whether sputum clearance practice was completed (for example: yes/no).

### dyspnea_score
Self-reported shortness of breath score.

### fatigue_score
Self-reported fatigue score.

### spo2_rest
Resting oxygen saturation, if available.

### heart_rate_rest
Resting heart rate, if available.

### notes
Optional free-text notes for symptoms, tolerance, or other observations.

## Note
This is a starter field guide and may be adapted for local educational or workflow needs.
